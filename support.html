Common Questions

How does the FODMAP analysis work?

Tolera uses a Cloudflare-hosted backend that combines a curated FODMAP food database with AI-powered meal analysis. When you log a meal, it identifies ingredients and calculates per-serving FODMAP loads.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your meals, symptoms, and tolerance profile stay on your device and optionally sync via your personal iCloud. See our privacy policy for details.

Why does Tolera need HealthKit access?

HealthKit access is optional. If granted, Tolera reads sleep, heart rate variability, exercise, and menstrual cycle data to help correlate them with your gut symptoms. None of this data leaves your device.

What is the three-phase FODMAP protocol?

Developed by Monash University, the protocol has three phases: Elimination (2–6 weeks of strict low-FODMAP eating to establish a baseline), Reintroduction (testing each FODMAP group one at a time to identify triggers), and Personalization (a long-term diet built around your specific tolerances).

Can Tolera replace medical advice?

No. Tolera is an informational tool. Always consult a registered dietitian or physician before making significant dietary changes, especially if you have a diagnosed condition.
