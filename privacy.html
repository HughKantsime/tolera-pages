Tolera Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Tolera is designed with privacy as a foundational principle. This policy explains what data Tolera handles and how.

Data We Collect

Tolera does not require an account. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Meals you log (photos, descriptions, FODMAP data)
  • Symptoms you record
  • Your FODMAP protocol phase and tolerance profile
  • Optional HealthKit data (sleep, heart rate variability, exercise, menstrual cycle) — only if you grant permission

iCloud Sync

If enabled, your data syncs across your devices via Apple iCloud (CloudKit) using your personal iCloud account. Apple manages this storage; Tolera does not have access to your iCloud data and cannot read it.

AI Meal Analysis

When you ask Tolera to analyze a meal photo or text description, that content is sent to a Cloudflare Worker that we operate. The Worker forwards the request to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis and returns the results to your device. We do not log, store, or retain meal photos or descriptions on our servers. A per-device identifier is used solely for rate limiting.

Barcode Lookups

When you scan a barcode, Tolera queries Open Food Facts and the USDA FoodData Central API through our Cloudflare Worker. Only the barcode itself is sent — no personal data.

HealthKit

If you grant HealthKit permission, Tolera reads health data on-device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings.

Analytics

Tolera does not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not collect device fingerprints, advertising identifiers, or behavioral data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone.

Your Rights

You can delete all Tolera data at any time by uninstalling the app, which removes all local storage. To remove iCloud data, use Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage → Tolera.
